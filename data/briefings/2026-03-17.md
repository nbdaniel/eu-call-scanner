**Why it fits AMPE:**
This is the core call for AMPE's profile. European NGO-led KA2 Cooperation Partnerships in Youth are built precisely for organisations like AMPE: non-formal education methodology, transnational working, youth-focused thematic expertise, and an established partner network across Erasmus+ countries. AMPE is eligible as lead applicant, and this action type sits squarely within AMPE's existing competence and project portfolio.

**Key Requirements:**
- Minimum number of partners across Erasmus+ countries (exact threshold to be confirmed from the 2026 Programme Guide)
- At least one partner from a different Erasmus+ country; European NGO applicant must coordinate
- Concrete joint outputs with documented non-formal education methodology
- All partners must hold active legal status and meet Erasmus+ eligibility

**Critical Unknowns to Resolve This Week:**
- Deadline date — without this, preparation cannot be scoped
- Grant ceiling — standard KA2 Cooperation Partnerships for European NGOs have historically ranged from €100,000–€400,000, but 2026 parameters must be confirmed from the Programme Guide or ANPCDEFP (Romanian National Agency)

**Next Steps:**
1. Contact ANPCDEFP this week to confirm deadline and budget envelope for ERASMUS-YOUTH-2026-PCOOP-ENGO
2. Review the 2026 Erasmus+ Programme Guide (Part B, KA2 section) once published
3. Begin internal scoping: which existing partners are confirmed and available for 2026 cooperation projects
4. Prepare a one-page concept note so that partnership conversations can begin immediately once deadline is confirmed
