### 1. Dezvoltarea unei rețele pentru tineret — NEETs, ITI Delta Dunării
**Programme:** ESF+ | **Score:** 82 — Excellent Match | **Recommendation: Apply**
**Deadline:** 26 May 2026 (69 days) | **Budget:** Not published — verify immediately
**URL:** [fonduri-structurale.ro](https://www.fonduri-structurale.ro/finantari/2094/dezvoltarea-unei-retele-pentru-tineret-care-sa-furnizeze-servicii-personalizate-si-de-calitate-tinerilor-cu-precadere-din-categoria-nee-ts-teritoriul-iti-delta-dunarii)

**What it is:** A competitive ESF+ call under the PEO programme targeting the ITI Delta Dunării territory, funding the creation of a youth network that delivers personalised, quality services to young people — with a strong focus on NEETs.

**Why it fits AMPE:** Direct thematic alignment with youth employment, NEET integration, and social inclusion — areas where AMPE has demonstrated ESF+ delivery capacity. NGOs are explicitly eligible as applicants or co-applicants. The ITI Delta Dunării territory is accessible from Craiova and falls within AMPE's operational South/South-West Romania reach.

**Key requirements to resolve this week:**
- Confirm whether AMPE can be **lead applicant or must partner** with a local ITI-territory entity (e.g., a youth centre or public authority from Tulcea/Constanța counties)
- Obtain the **Ghidul Solicitantului** (applicant guide) — check MFE and OI responsible for ITI Delta Dunării
- Clarify **co-financing rate** (standard ESF+ Romania: 2% for NGOs under PEO, but verify)
- Confirm **minimum/maximum grant amounts** — this is the single largest unknown

**Next steps:**
1. Download the full Ghidul Solicitantului from [mfe.gov.ro](https://mfe.gov.ro) or contact the managing authority (AM PEO / OI Delta Dunării) directly — this week
2. Map existing partners in the ITI territory; if none, contact Rețeaua Națională de Tineret (RNT) or local DGASPC for a co-applicant
3. Begin NEET targeting methodology documentation — the call specifies "personalised services," so prepare a needs-assessment framework
4. Allocate internal project development resources: 69 days is workable but leaves no slack
