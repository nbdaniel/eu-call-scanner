**Why this fits AMPE:**
This is the most natural call in the portfolio. KA2 ENGO-led cooperation partnerships in youth are AMPE's home territory — the action type rewards exactly what AMPE does: transnational youth work, non-formal education methodology, and structured international partnerships. As a Romanian NGO with European networks, AMPE is eligible to lead as applicant. The 2026 call year is current and active.

**What it funds:**
Transnational cooperation projects between at least 3 NGOs from different Erasmus+ programme countries, developing joint outputs in youth work, non-formal education, social inclusion, and intercultural dialogue. Typical grants in past years have ranged from €100,000–€400,000 over 12–36 months, but figures must be confirmed in 2026 call documentation.

**Key Requirements:**
- Minimum 3 partner organisations from at least 3 different Erasmus+ countries (verify exact threshold for 2026)
- Lead applicant must be a European NGO active in the youth field
- Project must produce tangible outputs (tools, methodologies, curricula, policy recommendations)
- All partners must hold valid PIC numbers and be registered in EACEA's participant portal

**Critical Gaps — Resolve This Week:**
- Deadline not yet published in the portal feed; the 2026 call guide may not be live yet
- Budget envelope (min/max grant) unconfirmed — check the 2026 Erasmus+ Programme Guide
