### 2. Dezvoltarea unei rețele pentru tineret — Servicii pentru tineri NEET, ITI Delta Dunării
**Programme:** ESF+ (PEO) | **Score:** 82 — Excellent Match | **Recommendation:** Apply — with conditions

**What it is:** A competitive ESF+ call under the Programme for Employment and Social Inclusion (PEO), targeting the development of a youth services network delivering personalised support to young people, particularly NEETs, within the designated ITI Delta Dunării territory (Tulcea County area). This is a Romanian structural funds call administered nationally.

**Why it fits AMPE:** Thematic alignment is very strong — youth services, NEETs integration, and network development are areas where AMPE has demonstrated expertise. NGOs are explicitly eligible. With a published deadline of **26 May 2026** (69 days from today), there is adequate preparation time if action starts this week.

**The critical issue:** AMPE is based in Craiova (Dolj County). The ITI Delta Dunării territory has strict geographic targeting — activities and beneficiaries must be located in that territory. AMPE cannot simply apply as a Craiova-based organisation delivering in Tulcea without either (a) establishing a formal operational presence there, or (b) entering the consortium as a non-lead partner alongside a Tulcea-based lead applicant. This is not a disqualifying factor, but it must be resolved before significant proposal work begins.

**Deadline:** **26 May 2026**
**Budget:** Not published (min/max null) — obtain the full Ghid al Solicitantului from [fonduri-structurale.ro](https://www.fonduri-structurale.ro/finantari/2094/dezvoltarea-unei-retele-pentru-tineret-care-sa-furnizeze-servicii-personalizate-si-de-calitate-tinerilor-cu-precadere-din-categoria-neets-teritoriul-iti-delta-dunarii)
**Partnership required:** Unknown — verify immediately.

**Key requirements to verify:**
- Whether AMPE can apply as lead or only as partner
- ESF+ co-financing rate (typically 2% for NGOs under Romanian ESF+ calls, but confirm)
- Eligible expenditure categories and performance indicators for youth network projects
- Which local Tulcea organisations could serve as lead or anchor partners

**Next steps:**
1. Download and read the full Ghid al Solicitantului this week — budget parameters, eligibility conditions, and partnership rules will all be there
2. Identify a credible partner organisation based in Tulcea County (local NGO, public youth centre, or AJOFM Tulcea) and make contact by end of week
3. If partnership route is viable, begin drafting a partnership agreement framework and project concept immediately — 69 days is sufficient but not generous for ESF+ proposals
